Tourist Talking Places Platform (1-month intensive course)
Goal
To enable participants to create, manage, and leverage interactive digital platforms that connect tourists
with local cultural, historical, and social narratives, promoting responsible tourism and meaningful
engagement in line with the Life Beyond Tourism model.
Course Purposes / Learning Outcomes
By the end of the course, participants will be able to:
 Develop and manage digital platforms that allow tourists to interact with local communities and
cultural sites.
 Design content that highlights local traditions, stories, and values, fostering cultural dialogue.
 Analyze visitor engagement data to improve the tourist experience and platform effectiveness.
 Promote ethical and responsible tourism practices through storytelling and interactive
experiences.
 Collaborate with stakeholders, including local communities, cultural institutions, and tourism
organizations.
Course Plan / Weekly Breakdown:
Week 1: Digital Platforms
 Principles of responsible tourism and cultural dialogue
 Overview of digital platforms in tourism
Week 2: Platform Development &amp; Content Creation
 Designing user-friendly interactive platforms
 Creating engaging content with cultural and historical value
 Integrating multimedia (text, audio, video, VR) for storytelling
Week 3: Community Engagement &amp; Promotion
 Engaging local communities and stakeholders
 Strategies to promote tourist participation and interaction
 Marketing and social media integration
Week 4: Monitoring, Evaluation &amp; Sustainability
 Analyzing user engagement and feedback
 Ensuring platform sustainability and ethical practices
 Presentation of platform projects and evaluation
