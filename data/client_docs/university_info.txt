Mission

The mission of Modern Education University (MEU) is to provide high-quality and accessible education
that equips students with practical, soft skills and theoretical knowledge.

Vision

Committed to inclusivity, innovation, and lifelong learning MEU integrates flexible online and face-to-
face programs to empower students and through strategic partnerships achieve excellence and
sustainability.

Values
Collaboration, Inclusivity, Innovation, Lifelong Learning, Excellence, Transparency, Equity and
Diversity, Social Responsibility, Sustainability
